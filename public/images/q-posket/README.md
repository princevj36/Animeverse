Place your 12 Q-Posket images in this directory with the following naming convention:

- `29.jpg` - Q-Posket Figure 29
- `30.jpg` - Q-Posket Figure 30
- `31.jpg` - Q-Posket Figure 31
- `32.jpg` - Q-Posket Figure 32
- `33.jpg` - Q-Posket Figure 33
- `34.jpg` - Q-Posket Figure 34
- `35.jpg` - Q-Posket Figure 35
- `36.jpg` - Q-Posket Figure 36
- `37.jpg` - Q-Posket Figure 37
- `38.jpg` - Q-Posket Figure 38
- `39.jpg` - Q-Posket Figure 39
- `40.jpg` - Q-Posket Figure 40

**Supported formats:** JPG, PNG, WEBP

**Recommended size:** 800x800px or larger (square format works best)

After placing the images, they will be automatically available at `/images/q-posket/[filename]`
